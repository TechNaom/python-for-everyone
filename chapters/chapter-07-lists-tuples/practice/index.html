Practice Bank: Lists & Tuples

A deeper set of practice problems, organized by topic, for extra reps beyond the main exercises — including scenario-based problems written in the same style you'll meet in real interviews. Uses everything from Chapters 1-6 plus this chapter's own toolkit: list literals, indexing/negative indexing/slicing, mutability, len(), the list methods from the lesson, tuples (creation, indexing/slicing, packing/unpacking), nested lists, and copying lists with .copy() or slicing. No dictionaries/sets, comprehensions, lambda, functions (def), or try/except yet.

On this page 1. Creating & Accessing 2. List Methods 3. Tuples 4. Nested Lists 5. Copying Lists 6. Real-World Lists & Tuples
