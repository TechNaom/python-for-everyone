Chapter 18 Practice Bank 70-90 min, hands-on

Practice Bank: Regular Expressions

A deeper set of practice problems, organized by topic, for extra reps beyond the main exercises — including scenario-based problems written in the same style you'll meet in real interviews. This is the chapter where the re module's pattern matching (match()/search()/fullmatch(), findall()/finditer(), character classes, quantifiers, anchors, groups & named groups, re.sub()/re.split(), re.compile(), greedy/non-greedy quantifiers, raw strings, and flags) becomes allowed, on top of everything from Chapters 1-17 (including generators, iterators, context managers, all OOP, exceptions, and file/CSV handling). No import beyond re/math/datetime/random/csv.

On this page 1. match()/search()/fullmatch() 2. findall() & finditer() 3. Classes, Quantifiers & Anchors 4. Groups & Named Groups 5. re.sub() & re.split() 6. Compiled Patterns 7. Bringing It Together
