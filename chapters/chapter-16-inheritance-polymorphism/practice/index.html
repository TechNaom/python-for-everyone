Practice Bank: Inheritance & Polymorphism

A deeper set of practice problems, organized by topic, for extra reps beyond the main exercises — including scenario-based problems written in the same style you'll meet in real interviews. This is the chapter where inheritance becomes allowed: class Child(Parent):, super().__init__(...), method overriding, polymorphism, and isinstance()/type() checks, on top of everything from Chapters 1-15 (including general classes, __init__, instance/class attributes, instance methods, dunder methods, @staticmethod, @classmethod, and @property). No import beyond math/datetime/random/csv.
